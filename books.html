Book Summaries

Love to read, but have no time? I've got you covered. On this page you will find links to my notes on books that I have read throughout my journey.


Can't Hurt Me (David Goggins)

The book in a nutshell:

This invaluable book, through David's journey, reveals the harsh truth of how legends are built - not by talent and luck, but by hard work and persistence. The 40% rule compells me to push past pain, and his journey taught me to embrace failure - using it to learn, grow and become stronger.

Get Book

Atomic Habits (James Clear)

The book in a nutshell:

his book helped me understand how habits are formed and what we can do to build long-lasting chains of cues, cravings, responses, and rewards to create systems that will help us achieve our goals. Often, we need only a slight tweak in the way we think to make a dramatic difference in action.

Get Book

A brief history of time(Stephen Hawking)

The book in a nutshell:

A predecessor to "brief answers to the big questions", this book took me through an eye opening journey through the universe and helped me connect the subject's teachings to the fundamental question we all ask: Why are we here?

Get Book

Brief answers to the big questions(Stephen Hawking)

The book in a nutshell:

This book helped me understand intuitively, the answers to some of the most compelling questions regarding the universe. It helped me realise that the unending thirst for these answers is the driving force to everything we do, and makes living exciting

Get Book

Elon Musk(Ashlee Vance)

The book in a nutshell:

Elon Musk is the first official biography of the creator of SolarCity, SpaceX and Tesla, based on over 30 hours of conversation time between author Ashlee Vance and Musk himself, highlighting his complicated childhood, the way he makes decisions and navigates the world, and how he managed to disrupt multiple industries, all with the goal of saving humanity.

Get Book

Steve Jobs(Walter Isaacson)

The book in a nutshell:

Steve Jobs is the most detailed and accurate account of the life of the man who created Apple, the most valuable technology company in the world.

Get Book
